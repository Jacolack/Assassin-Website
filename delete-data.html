Assassin App

Account Deletion Policy

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@assassin.fun with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your account email address in the message body for verification.

Data Deleted or Retained

  • Deleted: Personal details (e.g., name, email, and preferences).
  • Retained: User generated content.

Steps to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@assassin.fun with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your account email address in the message body for verification.
